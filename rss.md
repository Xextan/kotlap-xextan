- **Use an RSS reader**. Here are the recommended ones for each platform, which I chose based on the ability to send push notifications:
    - Windows: [Fluent Reader](https://hyliu.me/fluent-reader/)
    - Android:
        - [Capy Reader](https://capyreader.com/) ([F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/packages/com.capyreader.app/), [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/jocmp/capyreader/releases))
        - [Feeder](https://news.nononsenseapps.com/) ([F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/packages/com.nononsenseapps.feeder/), [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/spacecowboy/Feeder/releases/tag/2.18.0))
